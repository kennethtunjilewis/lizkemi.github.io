Institutional Lending Portfolio

LizKemi Capital Ltd deploys a robust suite of diversified capital structures designed to support individual scaling and corporate liquidity execution worldwide.

✦ Term Loan

A classic lump-sum funding distribution accompanied by structured, predictable repayment intervals. Ideal for direct equipment acquisition or defined corporate expansion tracks.

✦ SBA Loan

Government-backed commercial credit options offering small enterprises access to preferential rates and long-term liquidity protection safeguards.

✦ Business Line of Credit

On-demand revolving operational equity allocations. Borrow only what your business requires, repay it, and pull capital back instantly as corporate opportunities arise.

✦ Equipment Financing Loan

Asset-backed lending strategies where the equipment itself serves as security, enabling rapid mechanical or technology infrastructure provisioning without massive upfront expenditure.

✦ Revenue Based Financing Loan

Flexible capital injections tied to your monthly sales velocities. Repayments fluctuate seamlessly alongside your gross revenues to support variable seasonal business cycles.

✦ Micro Loan

Targeted, localized mini-capital injections structured specifically to accelerate startups, student entrepreneurial targets, and community storefront micro-ventures.

✦ Macro Loan

Specialized institutional macro-structural lending frameworks tailored for high-tier venture capital alignments, systemic industrial upgrades, and large corporate development tracks.

✦ Online Trading Loan

High-performance leverage accounts structured explicitly for capital market execution across modern liquidity pipelines: **Stocks**, **Forex**, and digital **Crypto assets** portfolios.

✦ Invoice Factoring Ledger

Convert outstanding customer receivables instantly into direct working capital by leveraging your unpaid commercial invoices for immediate liquidity injection.

✦ Commercial Real Estate Loan

High-tier funding instruments tailored for enterprise acquisitions, commercial property developments, or corporate land-use expansions across emerging regional lanes.

✦ Merchant Cash Advance (MCA)

Rapid, non-dilutive asset distributions offset against future credit card sales batches. Optimized for rapid inventory acquisition or immediate operating runway security.

✦ Project Finance Frameworks

Highly structured long-term financial architectures where debt redemption is strictly financed by the organic output yields of the developed project itself.
